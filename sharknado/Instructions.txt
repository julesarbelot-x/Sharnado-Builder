SHARKNADO BUILDER INSTRUCTIONS 

Step 1 :
Draw the profile of your shark
Step 2:
Draw the fins of your shark (left click to rotate, S and W to move the drawing plane and scroll wheel for zoom in and out)
Step 3:
Click T to go in the Tornado menu. Then draw your tornado
Step 4:
Click Q to place the sharks in the tornado
Step 5: 
Click SPACE to start the animation. 

At any point you can use R to delete objects.
